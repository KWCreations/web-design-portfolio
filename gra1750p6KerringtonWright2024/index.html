Join Hiccup and friends in the Isle of Berk as they embark on an incredibly exciting and unpredictable journey!

In a world where dragons lurk and fly about, vikings are trained to kill and hunt them in pursuit of their own survival. Hiccup, son of chief Stoic the Vast, works to find his own way of dealing with things after a certain terrifying but thought-provoking encounter. Though lacking strength and bravery, through his curiosity and wits he traverses this world's biggest challenges.
